Overview

Wonderla is a chain of amusement parks in India, with parks located in Bangalore, Kochi, and Hyderabad. It is one of the largest and most popular amusement park chains in the country, offering a wide range of thrilling rides, water slides, and attractions for visitors of all ages.

Wonderla was first established in Bangalore in 2005 and has since expanded to other cities. The park aims to provide a fun and entertaining experience for families and individuals, with a focus on safety and quality. It offers a variety of rides and attractions, including roller coasters, water rides, family rides, and kids' rides, ensuring there is something for everyone to enjoy.

Rides

  • Roller Coasters: Recoil, Insanity, Wonderla Bamba, Twister
  • Water Rides: Boomerang, Harakiri, Wave Pool, Rain Disco
  • Family Rides: Pirate Ship, Termite Coaster, Crazy Cars, Sky Wheel
  • Kids' Rides: Mini Venice, Jumping Frogs, Kiddies Wheel, Convoy
  • Thrill Rides: Maverick, Equinox, Techno Jump, Dropzone

Attractions

  • Live Shows: Dance Performances, Magic Shows, Acrobatics
  • Food and Dining: Restaurants, Snack Bars, Food Stalls
  • Virtual Reality: 4D Theater, XD Max, XD Cinema
  • Special Events: Festivals, Theme Nights, Celebrations
  • Water Park: Wave Pools, Lazy River, Water Slides
  • Adventure Activities: High Rope Courses, Ziplines, Bungee Jumping
  • Gaming Zones: Arcade Games, Virtual Reality Games

Others

In addition to the amusement park, Wonderla also offers resort facilities for visitors who want to extend their stay. The resorts provide comfortable accommodation, dining options, and additional recreational activities for a complete vacation experience. Wonderla is committed to providing a safe and enjoyable environment for its guests and follows strict safety protocols and guidelines.
